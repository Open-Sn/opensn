    "## Post-processing: Prompt-Only Growth Check\n",
    "For this simple one-group setup we compare the fission-production fraction to:\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\\frac{P(t)}{P(0)} = e^{\\alpha t}, \\quad \\alpha = \\nu\\Sigma_f - \\Sigma_a$$\n",
    "\n",
    "In the notebook, we evaluate this over the transient interval $$\\Delta t = t_1 - t_0$$:\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\\text{ratio}_{\\mathrm{analytic}} = e^{\\alpha\\Delta t}$$\n",
    "\n",
    "Code equivalent:\n",
    "\n",
    "```python\n",
    "ratio_analytic = math.exp(alpha * delta_t)\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "The numerical fraction is computed directly from fission production:\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\\text{ratio}_{\\mathrm{numeric}} = \\frac{\\text{fp\\_end}}{\\text{fp0}}$$\n",
    "\n",
    "Code equivalent:\n",
    "\n",
    "```python\n",
    "ratio_numeric = fp_end / fp0 if fp0 > 0.0 else 0.0\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "Then we compare\n",
    "$$\\text{ratio}_{\\mathrm{numeric}}\\text{ vs. }\\text{ratio}_{\\mathrm{analytic}}$$.\n"
